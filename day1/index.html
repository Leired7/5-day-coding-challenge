Feathers - the Penguin of Antarctic

We will tell you more about Feathers

“If ever the difficulties of your life seem overwhelming, consider the prospect of being eaten alive by savage penguins and rejoice that such horrors are unknown to you.” A.L. Kennedy

Feathers, like majority of her family and relatives, lives in the Southern Hemisphere. That is why your chances to be eaten alive by her are not that high. Feathers leads an ordinary penguin life at first sight. If you read about the penguins, you find information like: "aquatic flightless birds", "adapted for life in the water", "feed on fish" and so on. However, Feathers is different❕ That is why she has a whole website for herself!

  • Feathers can actually fly. She was extremely determined to learn this new skill and she did it!
  • Not only Feathers is adapted to life in water, she has also won the "Swim alongside Patagonia 2020" marathon where 267,367 penguins participated!
  • Feathers goes vegan one day a week to maintain her healthy life-style.
